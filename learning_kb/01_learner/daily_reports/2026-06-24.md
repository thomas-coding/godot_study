## Learner Understanding Confirmed

- `EventTrigger` is an invisible `Area2D` region that emits event data when the Player enters.
- `main.tscn` decides where the trigger is placed and which `event_id/target_action` it carries.
- `main.gd` decides what to do after receiving the event.
- `match` is a GDScript keyword similar to `switch/case`, used here to dispatch `target_action`.
- `triggered_event_ids` is a scene-level guard; `has_triggered` is only the trigger-local guard.
